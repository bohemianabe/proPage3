About Me

Welcome to my page, my name is Abel, and I'm an upcoming web developer in the DC area transitioning from the field of accountanting. For a few years now I've dabbled with font end coding such as html & CSS, but never found the right time to dedicate myself to learning more. That all changed when I found myself let go in April at the start of the downturn due to the pandemic (still a little weird writing that). I decided to commit myself more seriously, and what started with some more advanced lessons quickly evolved into a coding bootcamp as a full stack developer.

I'm diving in both feet on this new adventure, and my only regret is that I didn't do it sooner. I appreciate you visiting my site, and I hope we can work together soon.
